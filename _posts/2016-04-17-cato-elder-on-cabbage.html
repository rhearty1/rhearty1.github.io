--- layout: post title: Cato the Elder, on Cabbage date: '2016-04-17T14:00:00.001-04:00' author: RyGuy tags: modified_time: '2016-04-17T14:00:06.916-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2767138550438691941.post-8830109432055363285 blogger_orig_url: https://muchtoilingant.blogspot.com/2016/04/cato-elder-on-cabbage.html --- It is the cabbage which surpasses all other vegetables. It may be eaten either cooked or raw; if you eat it raw, dip it into vinegar. It promotes digestion marvelously and is an excellent laxative, and the urine is wholesome for everything. If you wish to drink deep at a banquet and to enjoy your dinner, eat as much raw cabbage as you wish, seasoned with vinegar, before dinner, and likewise after dinner eat some half a dozen leaves; it will make you feel as if you had not dined, and you can drink as much as you please.

De Agricultura, 156
