I wrote this after the greatest day in Cape May, August 2010

Cape May Day: Thursday 8/26/10
Wake up, waiting for Dad to arrive, hanging out with Lauren, Mike, Shannon, Mom
Dad arrives around 10:30, we go to Mad Batter for Lunch, Lauren gets her blueberry pancakes
We go over to the mall for a while and walk around there
Head over to the beach finally (1 pm), Lor and I play in the water, go for a little walk
Joe arrives and joins us in the water with Dad
we throw each other around in the water
we leave the beach around 5:30, people go into the pool for a quick dip
everybody takes a long time to change and get ready, I play robo defense and they yell at me
We go to Lobster House, but too late and there's no parking and a huge line for food
we get eaten by mosquitoes while enjoying our delicious fried food
Lor loves fried scallops and shrimp cocktail
Dad leaves from Lobster House, Mike leaves after we get him his books
The four of us go to watch E.T. at around 9:45 on the beach.
We finish it, Joe and I get teary-eyed from the Wagneresque soundtrack
Lor and I go for a long walk down to the cove, talk about our possible future together
she gives me the 20 second kiss she owes me
we go to a bar (Cabanas) with Shan and Joe and Mom (I have a video of this)
We walk home, go to bed and have a pillow fight, then we fall asleep (exhausted)
Best day ever
